Common skin problems include acne, eczema, psoriasis, rashes, and fungal infections.
Acne is caused by clogged pores, excess oil, bacteria, and hormonal changes.
Keep skin clean and moisturized, and avoid touching your face frequently.
Use sunscreen daily to protect skin from UV damage and prevent skin cancer.
Eczema causes dry, itchy, and inflamed skin and is often triggered by allergens or stress.
Fungal infections like ringworm or athlete's foot require antifungal creams or medications.
Avoid sharing personal items like towels or razors to prevent skin infections.
A diet rich in vitamins A, C, and E supports healthy skin.
Drink plenty of water to keep skin hydrated from the inside.
See a dermatologist for persistent rashes, unusual moles, or skin that does not heal.
