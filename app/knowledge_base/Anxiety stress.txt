Anxiety and stress are natural responses to pressure but can become harmful if chronic.
Symptoms include excessive worry, restlessness, rapid heartbeat, sweating, and difficulty sleeping.
Common triggers include work pressure, financial problems, relationship issues, and health concerns.
Deep breathing exercises and mindfulness meditation can calm the nervous system quickly.
Regular physical activity like walking, yoga, or cycling significantly reduces stress levels.
Maintain a balanced diet as poor nutrition can worsen anxiety and mood disorders.
Avoid excessive caffeine and alcohol as they can increase anxiety symptoms.
Talk to a trusted friend, family member, or mental health professional about your feelings.
Journaling thoughts and practicing gratitude can shift focus from stress to positivity.
Seek professional help if anxiety interferes with daily life, work, or relationships.
