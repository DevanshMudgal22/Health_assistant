Back pain is one of the most common health complaints affecting people of all ages.
It can result from muscle strain, poor posture, prolonged sitting, or injury.
Lower back pain is most common and often resolves within a few weeks with proper care.
Applying ice in the first 48 hours and then heat can help reduce pain and inflammation.
OTC pain relievers like ibuprofen or paracetamol may provide short-term relief.
Gentle stretching and low-impact exercises like walking or swimming aid recovery.
Maintain good posture while sitting, standing, and lifting heavy objects.
Avoid prolonged bed rest as it can worsen back pain over time.
A firm mattress and proper sleeping position help prevent back problems.
Seek medical help if back pain radiates down the leg, causes numbness, or follows an injury.
