The liver is a vital organ responsible for detoxification, digestion, and metabolism.
Common liver conditions include fatty liver disease, hepatitis, and cirrhosis.
Symptoms of liver problems include jaundice, abdominal pain, fatigue, and dark urine.
Excessive alcohol consumption is one of the leading causes of liver damage.
Maintain a healthy weight as obesity is a major risk factor for fatty liver disease.
Eat a balanced diet rich in vegetables, fruits, and whole grains to support liver function.
Avoid unnecessary medications and supplements as they can overburden the liver.
Get vaccinated against hepatitis A and B to prevent viral liver infections.
Avoid sharing needles or unprotected contact with infected blood to prevent hepatitis C.
Regular blood tests to check liver enzymes are recommended if you are at risk.
