Heart disease refers to a range of conditions affecting the heart including coronary artery disease and heart failure.
Common symptoms include chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, and irregular heartbeat.
Risk factors include high blood pressure, high cholesterol, smoking, obesity, and diabetes.
A heart-healthy diet includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins while limiting saturated fats.
Regular aerobic exercise such as walking or cycling strengthens the heart muscle.
Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption to reduce the risk of heart disease significantly.
Stress management through relaxation techniques and adequate sleep supports heart health.
Medications like statins, aspirin, or beta-blockers may be prescribed to manage heart conditions.
Regular health checkups including cholesterol and blood pressure monitoring are essential.
Call emergency services immediately if you experience chest pain, jaw pain, or sudden dizziness as these may signal a heart attack.
