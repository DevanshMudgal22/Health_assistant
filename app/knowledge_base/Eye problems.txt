Common eye problems include dry eyes, conjunctivitis, eye strain, cataracts, and glaucoma.
Eye strain often results from prolonged screen time and causes headaches, blurred vision, and fatigue.
Follow the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
Dry eyes can be relieved with artificial tear drops and by blinking more frequently.
Conjunctivitis (pink eye) is contagious and requires proper hygiene and sometimes antibiotic drops.
Protect eyes from UV rays by wearing sunglasses outdoors.
A diet rich in omega-3 fatty acids, vitamin A, and lutein supports good eye health.
Avoid rubbing your eyes as it can introduce bacteria and worsen irritation.
Get a comprehensive eye exam at least once every one to two years.
Sudden vision loss, severe eye pain, or seeing flashes of light requires immediate medical attention.
